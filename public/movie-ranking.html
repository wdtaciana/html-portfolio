The Best Movies According to Taciana

My top 3 movies of all-time.

El Secreto de tus Ojos

This movie is a masterpiece. Full of Plot twist, this one is the best movie for how likes to be surprise in the beginning, in the middle and in the end of the movie.

La Piel que Habito

In the same style as the previous film, this movie is also an excellent choice for people who like gripping movies with an unexpected ending

Once Upon a Time in Hollywood

You can't talk about good movies without mentioning Tarantino. His latest creation is an excellent movie for those who like parallel stories and references to events that really happened in the past.
